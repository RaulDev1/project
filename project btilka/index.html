Our Company

Jining Baolin Glass Product Co.,Ltd is a leading glass bottle manufacturer in China. We can produce more than 2000 kinds of glass bottles. Our main products are wine glass bottle, cosmetic glass bottle, beverage glass bottle, craft glass bottle, pharmaceutical glass bottle and so on.
